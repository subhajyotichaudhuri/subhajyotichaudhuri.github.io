The theoretical study of electron transfer encompasses a wide range of phenomena, from simple redox reactions to complex processes in biological systems and materials science.

`Problems of interest`<br>
- Accurate electron transfer rate predictions in large molecules
- Vibronic effects on electron transfer rates
- Ciral-induced spin selectivity
- Electron transfer in strongly coupled systems
- Ab-initio modeling of electrochemistry / charge transfer at interfaces
- Long-range electron transfer
- Designing reactive force fields for charge-transfer reactions
