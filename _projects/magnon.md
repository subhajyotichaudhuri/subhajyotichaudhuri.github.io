Magnonics is an emerging field that investigates the manipulation and control of magnons, the quanta of spin waves in magnetic materials, for information processing and energy-efficient computing.

`Problems of interest`<br>
- Building electronic structure based models for magnons
- Spin wave excitations in magnetic materials
- Magnon transport in magnetic nanostructures
- Magnon-photon-phonon interactions
- Spin logic
- Collective spin excitations in soft matter systems
