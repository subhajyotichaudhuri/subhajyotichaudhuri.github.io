Plasma-driven chemistry, which involves the use of high-energy, ionized gases presents several opportunities to initiate, perform, and control chemical reactions in both gas and solution phases.

`Problems of interest`<br>
- Plasma generated redox species
- Solvated electrons in solvents
- Plasma-induced chemical reactions
- Plasma-enhanced catalysis
- Out-of-equilibrium solvation models
- Excited-state reactions
